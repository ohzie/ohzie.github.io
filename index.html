Who We Are

A Leap Of Faith is a nomadic PVP corporation in The Initiative. We don't anchor to a single corner of the map. We stage where the content is - nullsec, Pochven, wormhole space, wherever a fight is waiting - and we're just as comfortable in your backyard as anywhere else in New Eden.

We've been active since late 2023 and have grown into one of the more active corps in INIT, consistently punching above our weight across fleet ops, blops, and small-gang content.
